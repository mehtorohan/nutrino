BMR Calculator

Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR) is the energy your body needs to function at rest.
The activity factor is a value used to estimate total daily energy expenditure (TDEE) by factoring in your activity level.

Common activity factors
sedentary (1.2)
lightly active (1.375)
moderately active (1.55)
very active (1.725)
extremely active (1.9 or higher).

To calculate TDEE, multiply your BMR by the activity factor that matches your activity level.

Remember that these are general guidelines, and individual variations exist.
For personalized advice, consult with a registered dietitian or healthcare professional.
